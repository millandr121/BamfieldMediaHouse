About the Council

We’re a local, volunteer-run non-profit dedicated to nurturing arts and culture in Bamfield and surrounding communities.

Our mission is to support creative expression and cultural connection through programs, events, exhibitions, and partnerships.

We welcome artists, makers, musicians, writers, youth, families, visitors, and anyone who wants to strengthen community through art.
